# Lead scoring tool 

## Overview
A comprehensive lead scoring and routing platform that centralizes lead capture from multiple channels, applies intelligent scoring algorithms, and automates qualification workflows. The tool serves as a pass-through system that ingests leads from HubSpot, Facebook Ads, Google Ads, phone systems, and other sources, then enriches and scores them for optimal sales team distribution.

## Problem
Difficulty tracking, scoring, and managing leads from multiple sources, with existing HubSpot lead scoring being insufficient for complex qualification needs based on company validation, contact verification, and business database cross-referencing

## Target User
Sales and marketing departments at companies with high-volume multi-channel lead generation

## Features
- Multi-channel lead ingestion API (HubSpot, Facebook Ads, Google Ads, Glowcom)
- Configurable lead scoring engine with email/phone validation
- Real-time contact verification and domain scoring
- Lead enrichment via business database lookups
- Automated lead routing based on scoring thresholds
